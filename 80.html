s
DEPENDING ON
is the same as
DEPENDING UPON.
Instead of WHAT, you can use WHO, WHEN, WHERE and HOW.
Depending on what she says, I'll be able to go.
Depending upon when I get the money, I will buy it.
Depending on where they go on vacation, she'll be happy.
Depending on how he can get the money, he'll go to college right away.
Depending upon on the situation, I'll decide if I should go.
This pattern can be made into a sentence using IT DEPENDS ON. It is usually
used as a short answer to a question.
Do you want to go?
It depends on the weather.
Can you give it to me?
It depends on how much you can pay.
Are you going to leave?
It depends on what he says.
Will you join them?
It depends upon when they go.
