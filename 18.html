PAST TENSE
In English, there are many different forms found in the past tense. In the beginning it may be difficult for
students to understand the nuances between the different forms. The best rule of thumb is to respond with the
same past tense pattern that a person speaks to you in. It will take time to get used to the subtle differences
between these patterns listed below.
There is a small table here which will indicate to you whether the pattern refers to the distant past, the most
recent past and up to the present.
