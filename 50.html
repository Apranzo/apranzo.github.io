2.
I DO NOTHING BUT v
.
This is another variation of
I ONLY v
.
There is no question form and isn't usually used in the future
tense. In the past tense, DO changes to DID. DOES is used in the third person singular in the present
tense. This pattern usually implies that you are dissatisfied with what the person did.
(present)
I do nothing but clean her room.. =
I only clean her room.
She does nothing but complain.
=
She only complains.
(past)
They did nothing but lay there.
=
They only laid there.
He did nothing but talk to her.
=
He only talked to her.
