You can use this in the present and future tense. In the third person singular in
the
present tense, it does not sound good. It does not sound good with any variation of COME, or
other variations of GO like GO IN, GO BACK etc. In the past tense, it can only be used in a
question or negative sentence (not a positive sentence).
We go dance there a lot.
He'll go meet her tomorrow.
I go play tennis there every day.
They'll go take the test with me.
Please go read that magazine.
She'll go explain it to him.
