2.
WHAT q
FOR?
Use only with verbs in positive questions. Do not use with negative questions or questions with adjectives. There
are some adjectives where it sounds all right (eg. famous).
However, for some reason with many adjectives it
sounds strange, so I suggest that beginning students don't use this with an adjective.
What did he come for?
=
Why did he come?
What does she want to bring it for?
=
Why does he want to bring it?
What did they call her for?
=
Why did they call her?
