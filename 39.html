IT'S WORTH IT.
You will hear this pattern sometimes used without a verb. It often refers to
whether the price of something is too high or too low, or whether the result of something you do
is satisfactory.

No, it isn't worth it.
Wasn't the trip worth it?
No, it wasn't worth it.
Was that watch worth it?
Yes, that watch was worth it.
